Osmani Tito-Corrioso received the Bachelor of Science in Mathematic degree from University of Oriente, Cuba in 2017, the Master of Science in Mathematical Science degree from University of Havana in 2022, and the Doctor in Science in Mathematical Science degree (Ph.D.) from University of Oriente in 2024.


He is Auxiliar Professor in the University of Matanzas, Cuba. His research interest area includes Post-quantum Cryptography, Cryptanalysis, Mathematical Modeling, etc.


Member of the Cuban Society of Mathematics and Computer Science, and of the Ibero-American Program of Science and Technology for Development, CYTED Network "New Cryptographic Tools for the e-community (New Crypto Tools)", Code 522RT0131.


Participated as Technical Evaluator for the National Council of Science and Technology (CONACYT) of Paraguay in 2023, in the framework of the Paraguayan Program for the Development of Science and Technology - PROCIENCIA II.


Spanish is his mother language; and speak, read and write technical English. Work with the following computer softwares: MATLAB, Maple, Wolfram Mathematica, LaTeX, Microsoft Office, C++, Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP).
